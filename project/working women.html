Nutrition for Working Women

Working women face unique challenges when it comes to nutrition. Balancing work, family, and personal health can be tough. Here are some tips:

  • Meal prep on weekends for busy weekdays.
  • Stay hydrated throughout the day.
  • Include a variety of fruits and vegetables in your diet.
  • Choose whole grains over refined ones.
  • Snack smart with nuts, yogurt, or fruit.
