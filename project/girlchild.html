Nutrition Tips

  • Stay hydrated!
  • Eat a balanced diet.
  • Balanced Diet: Aim for a variety of foods from all food groups—fruits, vegetables, whole grains, proteins, and healthy fats.
  • Healthy Snacks: Choose snacks that provide energy and nutrients, such as nuts, yogurt, fruit, or whole-grain crackers with hummus.
  • Calcium and Vitamin D: Important for bone health, especially during growth spurts.
  • Limit Added Sugars: Be mindful of sugary snacks and beverages.

Nutritional Needs for the Girl Child

Proper nutrition is vital for the growth and development of girls. Here are some key points:

  • Ensure a diet rich in fruits and vegetables.
  • Include whole grains for energy.
  • Provide adequate protein sources, such as beans and nuts.
  • Incorporate dairy products for calcium and vitamin D.
  • Encourage hydration and limit sugary drinks.
  • Educate about healthy eating habits.
  • Encourage the intake of roughage and dietary fiber.
  • Limit starchy foods.
  • Provide a limited amount of fats and carbohydrates.
